<h3>Beacons Take Flight</h3>
<p>Beacons Take Flight is a programme to provide every Beacon with at least
one opportunity to take a flight overseas for a school immersion or learning
journey. The objective of this programme is to develop global awareness,
cross-cultural skills and sensitivities.</p>
